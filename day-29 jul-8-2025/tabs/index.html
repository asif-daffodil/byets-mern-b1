Dhaka is the capital of Bangladesh. It is known for its vibrant culture and rich history. The city is a major economic, political, and cultural center in the country.
Rajshahi is known for its silk industry and mangoes. It is located in the northwest part of Bangladesh and is famous for its educational institutions.
Khulna is the gateway to the Sundarbans, the largest mangrove forest in the world. It is an important port city in southwestern Bangladesh.
Rangpur is known for its agriculture and is a major producer of crops like potatoes and rice. The city has a rich cultural heritage and is famous for its traditional music and dance.
